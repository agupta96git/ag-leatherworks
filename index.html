The Ethos

Built for the Pocket,
Not the Logo

AG Leatherworks was born from a simple observation: the world is full of bulky, over-designed wallets that prioritize logos over pocket-feel.

I believe your daily carry should be a silent companion — functional, slim, and crafted without the need for flaunting.

The Hand Behind the Stitch

Ashwani Gupta

I have always had the drive to "make," though for a long time, the right circumstances and resources were out of reach. Five years ago, those doors finally opened, and I haven't put my tools down since.

What began as a personal quest to build a better, slimmer wallet for myself has evolved into a deep respect for traditional craft. For me, leatherworking is just the beginning — as I continue to grow AG Leatherworks, I am also expanding my skills into woodworking and other disciplines, always chasing the goal of creating honest, handmade goods that stand the test of time.
